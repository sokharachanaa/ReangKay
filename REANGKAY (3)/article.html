Puberty

Understanding Body Changes During Puberty

Dr. Sopheak Chan

5 min read · Jul 2, 2026

Puberty is a natural stage of growth that every person goes through, usually starting somewhere between ages 9 and 15. It's your body's way of transitioning from childhood to adulthood, guided by hormones released from the brain.

During this time, you might notice your body growing taller quickly, changes in body shape, new hair growth, and shifts in your skin. These are all completely normal signs that your body is developing as it should.

Everybody grows at their own pace — and that's okay.

It's also normal to experience emotional ups and downs. Mood swings happen because your hormone levels are changing rapidly. Talking to someone you trust — a parent, teacher, or counselor — can help you feel more supported through these changes.

Remember: there's no "right" timeline for puberty. Some people start earlier, some later, and both are healthy. If you have any questions or concerns, our specialists are always here to help — anonymously if you prefer.
